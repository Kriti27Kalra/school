Mission

The mission of the school is to provide an environment that lays emphasis on intellectual and emotional growth in which every student discovers and realises his/her full potential and highest academic standards are achieved; where emphasis is laid on integrity, ethics, moral courage, hard work, responsibility and self-discipline; where activities focus on developing visionary, articulate and confident individuals with sensitivity and concern for the less fortunate, the environment, diversity and communal harmony.

Vision

Somerville International School aims to be an institution of excellence where sound ethical values, character, wisdom, leadership, service and academic achievement are encouraged in a safe and nurturing environment. Our vision extends beyond the classroom, instilling a sense of global citizenship and environmental stewardship. Committed to leveraging cutting-edge technology, we prepare students for the challenges of a rapidly evolving world. Together, we cultivate an inspiring and transformative educational journey that equips students for success and positive contributions to society.

About Us

Somerville International School

At Somerville International School, we believe in fostering an environment where students can explore their full potential and develop into well-rounded individuals.

We offer a wide range of academic programmes designed to cater to the unique needs and interests of our students. From Early Childhood Education to Primary, Secondary and Senior-Secondary levels, we provide a nurturing and stimulating learning environment. Our curriculum encompasses not only core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, Languages and Arts but also includes various Extracurricular Activities, Sports and Art programmes.

The mission of the school is to provide myriad opportunities to each child. The school believes that each child is unique and special and is blessed with immense potential. The endeavour is to guide each child as he/she charters a route to happiness and excellence. The school makes a concerted effort to ensure that as the students set out on this exciting voyage of discovery, they enjoy every moment of their innocence and appreciate the power of inner strength.
